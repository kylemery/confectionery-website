Our Chocolatiers

Rachel Augustin

Rachel has been the owner and manager of Coco's Chocolatier for 4 years now. Since she was little, Rachel has always been in love with every kind of chocolate. She's travelled the globe tasting cocoa and chocolate from over 26 countries. Her favourite kind of chocolate it dark with orange.

Marcelle Augustin

Marcelle, Rachel's older sister, has been in the chocolate business for over 14 years. She's the designer of the high-end chocolate treats in the store and has created over 150 kinds of white, milk, and dark chocolate desserts.
